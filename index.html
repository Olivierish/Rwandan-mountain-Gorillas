About Gorillas

Know about the Majestic World of Rwandan Mountain Gorillas

Discover the fascinating world of mountain gorillas, a species both rare and revered. These magnificent primates, known for their intelligence and emotional depth, reside in the dense forests of Rwanda's mountains. Adapting to life at high altitudes, they exhibit unique behaviors and social structures, living in closely-knit groups led by a dominant silverback. Their diet mainly comprises vegetation, showcasing their gentle nature despite their imposing size. In this section, we delve into their intriguing world, exploring their distinct characteristics, habitat, and the pivotal role they play in maintaining the ecological balance of their environment.
